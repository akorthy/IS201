About Me

This is what I do.

Hello! My name is Allison Korth, and welcome to my website! I grew up in California, and moved out to Utah for school. I love my family, as well as my dog, Rudy.

When I'm not studying for school, I stay very active. I love to snowboard, weight-lift, spin, do yoga, hike, and swim.

I am very passionate about the environment and climate change, as well as inequities within gender and race. I believe everyone should be treated justly, and that it is our job to act as stewards of this planet.
